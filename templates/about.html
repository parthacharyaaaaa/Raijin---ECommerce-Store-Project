Shipping Policy

At Raijin, we understand the importance of timely delivery and reliable shipping services. That's why we partner with trusted carriers to ensure that your order reaches you swiftly and securely. Upon placing your order, our team works diligently to process it with the utmost efficiency. Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days, barring any unforeseen circumstances or custom configurations. We offer both domestic and international shipping options to cater to our diverse clientele around the globe. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. For domestic orders, we provide standard and expedited shipping services, allowing you to choose the option that best suits your needs. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient. Rest assured, we strive to provide accurate tracking information for all orders, allowing you to monitor the progress of your shipment every step of the way. Should you have any inquiries regarding shipping or delivery, please don't hesitate to contact our dedicated customer support team, who will be more than happy to assist you..

Return Policy

At Raijin, customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we stand behind the quality of our products. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return policy to ensure your peace of mind. We accept returns of unused and unopened items within 30 days of the original purchase date. To initiate a return, simply contact our customer support team to obtain a return authorization and shipping instructions. Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part. Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it to ensure that it meets our return criteria. Upon approval, we will issue a refund to your original form of payment, minus any applicable restocking fees, within 5-7 business days. Please allow additional time for the refund to reflect in your account, depending on your financial institution. For items that are damaged, defective, or incorrectly shipped, we offer expedited replacements at no additional cost to you. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return policy, feel free to reach out to our dedicated customer support team, who will be happy to assist you every step of the way.
